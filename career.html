Careers

Our Group's success depends on our people's success and we provide the tools, resources and support to help you excel in the workplace.

It is often said that People are the greatest asset of a company. That is certainly true in our case where we work with cutting edge technology in challenging environments. Our goal is to cultivate a diverse workforce. Such a workforce is critical to meet the unique needs of the communities in which we operate.

We aspire to maximize the potential of our employees, develop them so that they are able to lead effectively and to grow the commercial viability of the companies in our group. It is only with the dedication of our people that we can serve our clients, both internal and external, and generate long-term value.

If you are looking for a business or technical role and are self-motivated, execution focused, driven and willing to work in a multicultural dynamic organization in challenging environments.
