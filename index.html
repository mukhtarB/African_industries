MISSION STATEMENT


To accomplish our mission, we are committed to uphold integrity, competence and teamwork as the way we do our business, We also believe in being innovative, technology driven and socially responsible in our quest for excellence.

VISION


At African Industries, our vision is to play a pivotal role in the development of the Nigeria where we operate, through the creation of jobs, introduction of innovation and creation of shareholder value. We aim to be the partner of choice through our entrepreneurial leadership, constant pursuit of excellence and high level of integrity.

VALUES


Quality & safety first, Monitoring continuous improvement through learning & development, Innovation & creativity in problem solving , An empowered, committed & motivated workforce with Excellence , Dynamism & Integrity.
